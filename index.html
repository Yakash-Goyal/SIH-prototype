Your Daily Dose of Tips

Take action to conserve groundwater and earn points for every water-saving step you take. Small changes can make a big difference — start saving today and get rewarded!

Tip:1

Collect and store rainwater to recharge groundwater levels and reduce dependency on external water sources.

Tip:2

Use drip irrigation or sprinkler systems to ensure minimal water wastage while irrigating crops.

Tip:3

A small leak can waste hundreds of liters of water daily. Regularly check your pipes, taps, and tanks for any leaks.
